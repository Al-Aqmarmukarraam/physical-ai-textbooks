Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This AI-Native textbook covers the fundamental concepts, technologies, and applications in the rapidly evolving field of physical artificial intelligence and humanoid robotics.

## About This Book

This textbook is organized into six comprehensive chapters, each containing three focused lessons:

1. **Introduction to Physical AI** - Understanding the fundamentals of AI systems that interact with the physical world
2. **Basics of Humanoid Robotics** - Exploring the design and control of human-like robots
3. **ROS 2 Fundamentals** - Learning the Robot Operating System for robotics development
4. **Digital Twin Simulation** - Mastering simulation tools like Gazebo and Isaac Sim
5. **Vision-Language-Action Systems** - Integrating perception, language understanding, and physical action
6. **Capstone: Simple AI-Robot Pipeline** - Building and deploying complete AI-robot systems

## Learning Approach

Each lesson follows a consistent format:
- **Introduction**: Context and relevance of the topic
- **Core Concept**: Detailed explanation of key ideas
- **Example**: Practical demonstration or application
- **Key Takeaway**: Essential points to remember

## How to Use This Textbook

This textbook is designed for both self-study and classroom use. You can navigate through chapters sequentially or jump to specific topics of interest. Each lesson is designed to be self-contained while building on previous concepts.
